# TicketPilot Billing & Subscription Guide

## Plans & Pricing

### What plans does TicketPilot offer?
| Plan | Price | Reps | Tickets/month | AI Responses |
|------|-------|------|---------------|--------------|
| **Starter** | $29/month | Up to 5 | 500 | 200 |
| **Growth** | $79/month | Up to 20 | 5,000 | 2,000 |
| **Enterprise** | Custom | Unlimited | Unlimited | Unlimited |

All plans include: multi-channel support, CASPER AI assistant, knowledge base, analytics dashboard, and email notifications.
Annual billing saves **20%** — equivalent to getting 2.4 months free.
All plans start with a **14-day free trial** — no credit card required.

### How do I upgrade my plan?
1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Upgrade Plan**.
2. Select the new plan.
3. Confirm payment — you are charged the prorated difference immediately.
4. New limits apply within 60 seconds.

**Downgrading** works the same way but takes effect at the end of your current billing cycle. You will not be charged for the cheaper plan until renewal.

### What happens if I exceed my ticket or AI response limit?
- **Tickets**: Additional tickets are accepted but processing may be queued during high load.
- **AI responses**: Once the monthly AI limit is reached, the AI assistant is disabled for the rest of the billing cycle. Reps can still reply manually. Upgrade to restore AI access immediately.

## Invoices & Payment

### How do I download an invoice?
1. Go to Settings → Billing → Invoice History.
2. Click **Download PDF** next to any invoice.
3. Invoices are also emailed to the billing contact automatically on each charge.

### How do I change the billing email or company name on invoices?
Go to Settings → Billing → Billing Details. Update the fields and click **Save**. The next invoice will reflect the new details. To update past invoices, contact billing@ticketpilot.io.

### My credit card was declined — what do I do?
1. Go to Settings → Billing → Payment Method → **Update Card**.
2. Enter the new card details and save.
3. Click **Retry Payment** to immediately attempt the failed charge.
4. If payment fails 3 times over 7 days, your account is downgraded to the free tier and tickets queue without AI processing.

### Do you support purchase orders or wire transfers?
Yes, for **Enterprise plans only**. Contact sales@ticketpilot.io with your PO number. Wire transfer details are provided in the invoice. Net-30 terms available for verified companies.

## Refunds & Cancellation

### Can I get a refund?
- **Within 7 days of first purchase**: Full refund, no questions asked. Email billing@ticketpilot.io with your account email.
- **After 7 days (annual plan)**: Prorated refund for unused months.
- **Monthly plan**: Non-refundable after the billing cycle starts. You retain access until the end of the paid period.
- **Free trial**: No charge, nothing to refund.

### How do I cancel my subscription?
1. Settings → Billing → Subscription → **Cancel Plan**.
2. Confirm cancellation — your access continues until the end of the current billing period.
3. After expiry, the account moves to read-only mode for 30 days (you can still export data).
4. After 30 days in read-only mode, all data is permanently deleted unless you reactivate.

### What happens to my data after cancellation?
- **Days 0–30 after expiry**: Read-only access, no new tickets accepted. You can export all data via Settings → Export → Full Export.
- **Days 31–60**: Data queued for deletion. Contact support to recover.
- **Day 61+**: All data permanently deleted and unrecoverable.

## Trial & Onboarding

### How does the 14-day free trial work?
- No credit card required to start.
- Full access to the Growth plan features for 14 days.
- At day 14, you are prompted to select a plan. If you don't, the account is paused (no new tickets or AI responses).
- Existing data is retained for 30 days after trial expiry.

### Can I extend my free trial?
Yes, one 7-day extension is available per account. Go to Settings → Billing → **Extend Trial** (visible while the trial is active). Alternatively, contact sales@ticketpilot.io for a demo-based extension.

### Is there a non-profit or educational discount?
Yes — 40% discount for verified non-profits and academic institutions. Email billing@ticketpilot.io with proof of status (charity registration number or .edu email domain). Discount applies from the next billing cycle.
