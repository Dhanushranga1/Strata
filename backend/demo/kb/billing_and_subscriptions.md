# Billing and Subscriptions — Help Guide

## Subscription Plans

We offer three subscription tiers:

**Starter Plan** — $9/month
- Up to 3 users
- 500 tickets per month
- Email support only
- Basic analytics dashboard

**Professional Plan** — $29/month
- Up to 15 users
- Unlimited tickets
- Email and live chat support
- Advanced analytics and reporting
- Priority response time (under 4 hours)

**Enterprise Plan** — $99/month
- Unlimited users
- Unlimited tickets
- Dedicated support representative
- Custom integrations and API access
- SLA guarantee with 1-hour response time
- Quarterly business reviews

## How to Upgrade or Downgrade Your Plan

To change your subscription plan:
1. Log in to your account and go to **Settings → Billing**.
2. Click **Change Plan**.
3. Select the new plan and confirm your choice.
4. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

You will receive a confirmation email once the plan change is processed.

## Billing Cycle and Invoices

- Subscriptions are billed monthly on the anniversary of your sign-up date.
- Annual billing is available at a 20% discount — contact support to switch.
- Invoices are emailed automatically each month and are also available under **Settings → Billing → Invoice History**.
- We accept all major credit and debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) and PayPal.

## Being Charged Twice

If you see a duplicate charge on your statement:
1. Check your email for two separate invoice receipts — this may indicate two active subscriptions.
2. Go to **Settings → Billing** and confirm you have only one active subscription.
3. If a duplicate charge is confirmed, contact our support team with your invoice numbers and we will issue a refund within 3–5 business days.
4. Refunds are returned to the original payment method.

## How to Cancel Your Subscription

To cancel your subscription:
1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Manage Subscription**.
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**.
3. Confirm the cancellation in the dialog box.

Your account will remain active until the end of your current billing period. After cancellation, your data is retained for 30 days before permanent deletion. You can reactivate your account within this window without losing any data.

## Failed Payments

If a payment fails:
- We will retry the charge automatically after 3 days and again after 7 days.
- You will receive an email notification for each failed attempt.
- After 3 failed attempts, your account will be paused. You can reactivate it by updating your payment method under **Settings → Billing → Payment Method**.

## Refund Policy

- Refunds are available within 7 days of a charge if you have not used the service during that period.
- Partial refunds are issued on a pro-rated basis for annual plans.
- To request a refund, contact our support team with your account email and invoice number.
- Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days.

## Changing Your Payment Method

To update your credit card or payment method:
1. Go to **Settings → Billing → Payment Method**.
2. Click **Update Payment Method**.
3. Enter your new card details and save.

Your next invoice will be charged to the new payment method automatically.

## Tax and VAT

- Prices shown are exclusive of applicable taxes.
- VAT is applied to customers in the European Union based on their billing country.
- If your organization is VAT-exempt, upload your VAT certificate under **Settings → Billing → Tax Information**.

## Promo Codes and Discounts

- Promo codes can be applied at checkout or under **Settings → Billing → Apply Promo Code**.
- Codes are single-use unless stated otherwise.
- Discounts do not stack with other promotions.
- For partnership or bulk pricing inquiries, contact our sales team.
