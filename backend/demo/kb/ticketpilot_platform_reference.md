# TicketPilot Platform Reference Guide — Features, Plans, and Policies

## Subscription Plans and Pricing

TicketPilot offers three subscription plans: Starter, Professional, and Enterprise.

**Starter Plan — $9 per month**
The Starter plan supports up to 3 users and allows 500 tickets per month. It includes email support only, a basic analytics dashboard, and standard ticket management features. The Starter plan does not include live chat support, API access, or custom integrations. Response time on the Starter plan is within 24 hours.

**Professional Plan — $29 per month**
The Professional plan supports up to 15 users and allows unlimited tickets per month. It includes email support and live chat support, an advanced analytics dashboard with full reporting, priority response time under 4 hours, and access to the AI assistant for support representatives. The Professional plan does not include dedicated account management or custom SLA agreements.

**Enterprise Plan — $99 per month**
The Enterprise plan supports unlimited users and unlimited tickets. It includes a dedicated support representative, custom integrations, full API access, a guaranteed SLA with 1-hour response time, quarterly business reviews, and Single Sign-On (SSO) configuration support. Enterprise customers receive a dedicated phone support line listed in their onboarding email.

## Differences Between Plans

The key differences between Starter, Professional, and Enterprise plans are:
- **Users**: Starter allows 3 users, Professional allows 15 users, Enterprise allows unlimited users.
- **Tickets**: Starter allows 500 per month, Professional and Enterprise allow unlimited tickets.
- **Support channels**: Starter has email only; Professional adds live chat; Enterprise adds a dedicated rep and phone line.
- **Response time SLA**: Starter is 24 hours, Professional is under 4 hours, Enterprise is under 1 hour.
- **AI assistant**: Available on Professional and Enterprise plans only.
- **API access**: Available on Enterprise plan only.
- **SSO**: Available on Enterprise plan only.
- **Price**: Starter is $9/month, Professional is $29/month, Enterprise is $99/month.

## How to Upgrade or Downgrade Your Plan

To upgrade or downgrade your subscription plan, go to Settings, then Billing, then click Change Plan. Select your new plan and confirm. Upgrades take effect immediately and you are charged a pro-rated amount for the remainder of the billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing period — you keep your current plan features until the next renewal date. You will receive a confirmation email when the plan change is processed successfully.

## Billing and Payment

TicketPilot bills subscriptions monthly on the anniversary of your sign-up date. Annual billing is available at a 20 percent discount by contacting our support team. We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and PayPal. Invoices are emailed automatically each month and are available under Settings, then Billing, then Invoice History.

To update your payment method, go to Settings, then Billing, then Payment Method, and click Update Payment Method. Your next invoice will be charged to the new card automatically.

If a payment fails, TicketPilot retries the charge after 3 days and again after 7 days. After 3 failed attempts, your account is paused. You can reactivate by updating your payment method.

## Refund Policy

Refunds are available within 7 days of a charge if the service has not been used during that period. Partial refunds are issued on a pro-rated basis for annual plans. To request a refund, contact support with your account email and invoice number. Refunds are processed within 5 to 7 business days and returned to the original payment method.

Duplicate charges are refunded within 3 to 5 business days after the issue is confirmed by our billing team. To report a duplicate charge, go to Settings, then Billing, and contact support with the two invoice numbers.

## Cancellation Policy

To cancel your subscription, go to Settings, then Billing, then Manage Subscription, and click Cancel Subscription. Confirm the cancellation in the dialog. Your account remains active until the end of the current billing period. After cancellation, your data is retained for 30 days. You can reactivate your account within that 30-day window without losing any data. After 30 days, all data is permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

## Data Retention and Account Deletion

After cancellation, customer data including tickets, messages, knowledge base documents, and user accounts is retained for 30 days. During this period you can export all data under Settings, then Data, then Export Data. After 30 days, data is permanently deleted. To request immediate account deletion, contact support — deletion is completed within 30 days of the request.

## File Attachments

Each file attachment has a maximum size of 10 MB. Supported file types are PDF, PNG, JPG, JPEG, GIF, DOCX, XLSX, TXT, and ZIP. You can attach up to 5 files per message. If an upload fails, verify the file is under 10 MB and is one of the supported types.

## Password Reset

To reset your password, go to the login page and click Forgot Password. Enter your account email address and click Send Reset Link. Check your inbox for a password reset email — the link is valid for 1 hour. If you do not receive the email within 5 minutes, check your spam folder. If the link expires, repeat the process to receive a new link. After clicking the link, enter and confirm your new password. The change takes effect immediately.

## Adding and Managing Users

To add a new user, go to Settings, then Team, then Invite Member. Enter the user's email address, select their role — Admin, Support Rep, or Customer — and send the invitation. Invitation links expire after 7 days. If the link expires, resend the invitation from the same page. New users must accept the invitation before they can access the platform. To remove a user, go to Settings, then Team, click Remove next to the user's name, and confirm. The user loses access immediately. Their historical activity is preserved in the audit log.

## Response Times and Support Hours

Professional plan customers receive a response within 4 hours during business hours. Enterprise customers receive a response within 1 hour at any time, including weekends. Live chat is available Monday to Friday, 9 AM to 6 PM IST, for Professional and Enterprise customers. Email support is available 24/7 for all plans.
