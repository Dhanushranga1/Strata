TicketPilot Billing & Subscription Guide

PLANS AND PRICING
TicketPilot offers three plans:
- Starter: $29/month — up to 3 agents, 500 tickets/month, email support
- Growth: $99/month — up to 15 agents, 5,000 tickets/month, AI responses, KB integration
- Enterprise: Custom pricing — unlimited agents, unlimited tickets, SLA guarantees, dedicated support

BILLING CYCLE
All plans are billed monthly on the anniversary of your signup date. Annual billing is available at a 20% discount. Invoices are sent to the account owner's email address on the billing date.

HOW TO UPGRADE OR DOWNGRADE
1. Go to Settings > Billing in your TicketPilot dashboard
2. Click "Change Plan"
3. Select your new plan and confirm
Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing period.

PAYMENT METHODS
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and PayPal. Bank transfers are available for Enterprise plans. All transactions are processed securely via Stripe.

REFUND POLICY
We offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for new subscriptions. Refunds are not provided for partial months or after the 14-day window. To request a refund, contact billing@ticketpilot.io with your account email and reason.

FAILED PAYMENTS
If a payment fails, we retry after 3 days and again after 7 days. You will receive an email notification for each failed attempt. After 3 failed attempts, your account will be downgraded to the free tier until payment is resolved.

CANCELLATION
You can cancel at any time from Settings > Billing > Cancel Subscription. Your account remains active until the end of the current billing period. Data is retained for 30 days after cancellation, after which it is permanently deleted.

TAX AND VAT
TicketPilot charges applicable taxes based on your billing address. EU customers with a valid VAT ID can enter it in Settings > Billing to receive VAT-exempt invoices.

ADDING SEATS
On the Growth and Enterprise plans, you can add agent seats at any time. Additional seats are billed at the per-seat rate prorated for the remainder of the billing period. Go to Settings > Team > Add Seat to add agents.
